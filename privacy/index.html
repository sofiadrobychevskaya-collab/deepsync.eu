Last updated: 21 July 2026

DeepSync ("DeepSync", "we", "us") operates deep-sync.eu and the DeepSync Proposal Evaluator. This policy explains what personal data the site processes, why, and how you can control it. The data controller is Sofia Drobyczewska, contactable at sofia@deep-sync.eu.

Who else sees this data

Form submissions are relayed through Google Apps Script / Google Sheets so DeepSync can review and reply to enquiries, and analytics runs on Google Analytics. If you use the Evaluator's opt-in "Deep AI Check", that request is relayed through a separate Google Apps Script endpoint to Anthropic's Claude API to generate the review. Google and Anthropic may process this data on servers outside the EU under their standard contractual clauses. We do not sell personal data or share it with anyone else.

How long we keep it

Enquiry and unlock-form data is kept only as long as needed to respond to you, or until you ask us to delete it. Analytics data follows Google Analytics' standard retention settings.

Your rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct or delete it
  • Restrict or object to how it's used
  • Receive a copy in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent at any time (this doesn't affect processing already carried out)
  • Lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority

To exercise any of these, email sofia@deep-sync.eu.

You can change your cookie choice at any time.
