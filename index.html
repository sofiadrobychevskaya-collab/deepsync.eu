About DeepSync

We turn European priorities into programmes that can be funded and delivered.

DeepSync is an EU programme development partner. We connect policy, research, public authorities, industry and investment to build credible concepts, capable consortia and practical pathways — from idea to implementation and scale.

01Policy to project

We understand what Europe is trying to achieve.

We translate EU priorities and call requirements into a clear project logic — with the evidence to back it up.

02Right ecosystem

We build around missing capabilities, not logos.

Our consortium work brings together the right scientific, industrial, public-sector and market partners — with real ownership and contribution, not just names on a page.

03Continuous intelligence

We combine data with professional judgement.

We track Commission evaluation patterns and funding data, but every project is shaped by expert review — not just automated matching.

Open to international cooperation

We welcome collaboration with universities, innovation agencies, public authorities, accelerators, VC and CVC funds, companies and expert networks seeking to connect European funding with real deployment and follow-on investment.
