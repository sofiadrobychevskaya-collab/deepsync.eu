About DeepSync

We turn European priorities into programmes that can be funded and delivered.

DeepSync is a founder-led EU programme development company supported by an international specialist network. We connect policy, research, public authorities, industry and investment to build credible concepts, capable consortia and practical pathways from an idea to implementation and scale.

01 / POLICY TO PROJECT

We understand what Europe is trying to achieve.

We translate work programmes, call requirements, policy priorities and evaluator expectations into a clear project logic and evidence plan.

02 / RIGHT ECOSYSTEM

We build around missing capabilities, not logos.

Our consortium work connects the right scientific, industrial, public-sector and market roles, supported by delivery ownership and measurable contribution.

03 / CONTINUOUS INTELLIGENCE

We combine data with professional judgement.

Commission evaluation patterns, CORDIS evidence, call intelligence, AI diagnostics and expert review create a stronger feedback loop than proposal writing alone.

Open to international cooperation

We welcome collaboration with universities, innovation agencies, public authorities, accelerators, VC and CVC funds, companies and expert networks seeking to connect European funding with real deployment and follow-on investment.

How can we work together?

Investment partnership

Building the bridge from EU funding to private capital.

Europe's innovation “valley of death” appears when promising R&D reaches the point where grants alone are not enough to commercialise and scale. The European Commission's startup and scaleup strategy calls for stronger venture-capital markets, more institutional investors and a faster route from lab to market. DeepSync is building that bridge in both directions.

For VC & CVC funds Qualified European innovation deal flow

Access grant-backed DeepTech companies and project results that we know through proposal, consortium and programme work. We help explain the public-funding history, technology maturity, consortium assets and remaining scale-up needs.

For portfolio companies EU funding fit before the next round

We assess which portfolio companies may fit Horizon Europe, EIC, Digital Europe and related instruments, identify evidence gaps and map the right grant, consortium and investor-readiness pathway.
