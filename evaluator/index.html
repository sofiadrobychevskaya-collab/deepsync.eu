EU PROPOSAL INTELLIGENCE

See your proposal through an evaluator's eyes.

Upload a Horizon Europe, Digital Europe or EIC proposal. DeepSync checks its logic against recurring European Commission evaluation patterns and shows what to strengthen before submission.

Every assessment is grounded in real European Commission evaluation comments and evidence from 100,000+ EU-funded CORDIS projects.

DeepSync does not merely review how your proposal is written. It identifies the evaluation patterns associated with successful and unsuccessful funding decisions — and shows precisely where your proposal is likely to gain or lose points.

Unlike general-purpose AI tools, DeepSync uses a proprietary database of Commission evaluation feedback that is not available in public AI training data. It combines these real evaluation patterns with call-specific requirements and evidence from previously funded projects to deliver a more precise, context-aware assessment.
